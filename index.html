Welcome!

This is the unofficial webapp of the University of Limerick, Ireland. You'll find some interesting non academic information about UL here. For all official communication and information please visit the official website

1970's

Campaign for University at Limerick begins,The Limerick University Project Committee was founded in September 1959 by the mayor of Limerick in 1957, Ted Russell .Founded in 1972 as the National Institute for Higher Education, Limerick.

1980's

In 1989, NIHE Limerick was established by legislation as the University of Limerick and NIHE Dublin was established as Dublin City University, each with the power to award its own degrees. These became the first institutes since Irish independence to be given the title "university".

1990's

Thomond College incorporated as department of educational and professional studies, focusing on secondary education.

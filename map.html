Some basic steps before getting started

Press the "Animate" button to Start or Stop the model animation.

Augmented Reality is currently only supported on an Android device click the button to enter AR mode.

Pinch to zoom in and zoom out on an Android device or use the mouse wheel on a Laptop or Desktop.

Drag with one finger to rotate the model.

Press the button in the Augmented Reality view to capture a photo, long press to record a video.

For all official information please visit Official website of UL.
